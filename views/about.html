About

Botanizer is a rare plant species reporting challenge and a way to find rare plants located around you. It's a free app perfect to take on a bushwalk or use for a school activity.

Points are awarded for uploading photos, correctly identifying plant species, and entering a description about the plant. The ultimate aim is to become a Botaniser!

Species reports are entered by the general public and uploaded after being vetted. If the report is correct, the uploader scores points and works their way towards becoming a Botaniser.

Botaniser was created on 13th July 2014 by four aspiring Botanisers from Perth, Western Australia.
